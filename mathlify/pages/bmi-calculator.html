BMI Calculator

Calculate your Body Mass Index using metric (kg/cm) or imperial (lbs/ft·in) measurements.

⚖️ BMI Categories
Underweight — BMI < 18.5
Normal — BMI 18.5 – 24.9
Overweight — BMI 25 – 29.9
Obese — BMI ≥ 30
ℹ️ About BMI

BMI is a simple screening tool based on weight and height. It doesn't measure body fat directly and may not apply to athletes, elderly people, or children.
